About AlphaBook

AlphaBook is a lightweight trading widget designed to be used in comparison with Alphas other features. Use AlphaBook to track all 72 Live Orderbooks on Binance Futures. Showing details such as the best bid/ask as well as the qty on either side. Use this in conjunction with the charting and orderbooks for each product to gain market perspective and see what coins are moving instantly without having to search through the website. As well as this the layout of AlphaBook can be fully customised and resized to your liking allowing you to group/seperate items into groups as you wish.
