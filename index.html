Ladder / Chart

At a Glance

The Ladder is a depiction of the current state of the BTCUSDT orderbook. This runs up and down $100 from the bid/ask to show the user the full depth of market. Here you can see the quantity at each price, supplemented by the threshold setting to highlight prices with greater size. Alongside this to the left of the orderbook is the Size Indicator. This is designed to show levels of importance based on the size present via a bar. To the right of the orderbook is the VAP (Volume Traded at Price) Indicator. This on startup will begin to tally the amount of BTC traded at each price respectively giving the user an idea of value at each price.

There are two types of execution possible via the Ladder. Firstly is directly going to market by using the buy or sell buttons. In addition to this you can click above the bids in the empty blue boxes to go long at market, or coincidingly go short by hitting the empty red boxes below the bid/ask. If you want to post a limit order into the orderbook this can be done also by clicking on a price on the bid or ask and this will highlight your limit in the order column. These limits can also be cancelled by double clicking them.
